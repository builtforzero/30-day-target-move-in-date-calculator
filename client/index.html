This tool is designed to help you set goals around a monthly Housing Placement rate to help drive an increase in your community's outflow.

Enter your data along with your targets. Then, press the GO! button to view your Suggested Reduction Goals.

PLEASE NOTE: There is no guarantee that reaching these Housing Placement and Outflow targets will result in a reduction in your community's Actively Homeless Number. The tool below uses a simple linear model that rests on the assumption that your monthly inflow and outflow are fairly constant each month and that the three months of data you enter below are representative of your typical inflow and outflow. Since it is impossible to accurately predict what your actual inflow and outflow will be in the future, these assumptions are non-trivial. Therefore, this tool is for goal-setting purposes only and should not be relied upon to provide an accurate projection of what will happen in your homelessness system.

EXPLANATION



To reduce your active list by people in month(s), you will need to reduce your Actively Homeless Number by people per month.


people / month(s)

= person monthly reduction

Assuming people are joining your list each month, you will need to have people move off your list each month.


person monthly reduction + person monthly inflow

= person monthly outflow needed

Assuming people are currently moving off the list each month, due to moving into housing and due to other outflow, you can increase your monthly housing placements to people per month to reach your goal.


person total outflow - current monthly housing placements

= person monthly other outflow


person monthly outflow needed - person monthly other outflow

= monthly housing placements needed

NOTE: This estimate is based on the assumption that your monthly Total Inflow and Other Outflow rates remain similar to the 3 month averages calculated above. Please bear in mind that past performance is not indicative of future performance, particularly for factors like Inflow and Other Outflow which are heavily impacted by external factors outside the control of your homelessness response system.
