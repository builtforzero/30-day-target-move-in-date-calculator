WHERE DOES THIS NUMBER COME FROM?

month(s) to reach your target

person reduction

To reduce your active list by people in month(s), you will need to reduce your Actively Homeless Number by people per month.

people / month(s) = person monthly reduction

average total monthly inflow

Assuming people join your list each month, you will need people to move off your list each month.

person monthly reduction + person monthly inflow = person monthly outflow needed

average total monthly outflow

average monthly housing placements

Assuming people are currently moving off the list each month, due to moving into housing and due to other outflow, you can increase your monthly housing placements to people per month to reach your goal.

person total outflow - current monthly housing placements = person monthly other outflow

person monthly outflow needed - person monthly other outflow = monthly housing placements needed

HOW WE MAKE THIS CALCULATION: This estimate is based on the assumption that your monthly Total Inflow and Other Outflow rates remain similar to the 3 month averages calculated above. Please bear in mind that past performance is not indicative of future performance, particularly for factors like Inflow and Other Outflow which are heavily impacted by external factors outside the control of your homelessness response system.
